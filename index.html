about

lexicontol paces your eyes by un-blurring words instead of flashing them one at a time.

The whole paragraph stays on screen in a normal horizontal layout. A reading "head" sweeps left-to-right at your target speed, sharpening words as it reaches them — so you keep natural eye movement while a pacer pulls you forward.

tune everything in s settings. paste your own text with n.
